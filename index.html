Why Smart Helmet

A life-saving sewer helmet equipped with advanced safety features to protect laborers from hazards,
ensuring their well-being during challenging working conditions.

Gas Detection

Identify toxic gases (NH3, SO2, H2S, Cl2, NOx)
in sewage environments.

Constant Health Monitoring

Continuous vitals tracking (temperature, pulse) ensures laborer well-being in confined spaces.

Immediate Alerts

Warn workers with visual and audible alarms
when gas levels become unsafe.

Remote Oversight

Supervisors remotely monitor worker conditions for timely assistance and safety.
